# Research Route Slim Activation Cases

The skill is intended for sustained or multi-stage paper work, durable state, venue or contribution decisions, bilingual argument revision, and transfer between agents or harnesses.

## Should activate

1. A multi-session humanities paper must compare neighboring literature, stabilize a contribution, and leave canonical state for another agent.
2. A social-science manuscript needs evidence reconciliation, thesis revision, and a venue-fit decision before submission.
3. A bilingual paper is moving between Spanish and English and needs a terminology ledger plus durable handoff.

## Should not activate by default

1. Isolated proofreading of a supplied paragraph.
2. Citation formatting in an already settled bibliography.
3. A one-off summary of a supplied article.
4. A narrow lookup for one fact or citation.

The non-trigger cases may activate only when the user explicitly names Research Route or asks for durable project state.
